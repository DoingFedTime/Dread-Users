by /u/Beelzebub 2 days ago in /d/Dread
I saw a post on /d/OpSec that had been approved by another mod. The post asked for suggestions on how to get a product delivered from a high-risk country.

I've returned the post to the queue under the " use common sense, don't be an idiot" section. I've given the poster "Possibly LE" as a Flair.

I'd prefer you add it to the site wide rule. OpSec only has one rule, and I like it that way.
